How to Make a camel

Interesting facts about camels
  • Camels have thick lips which help them forage thorny plants which other animals can't eat
  • Camels can shut their nostrils completely during sandstorms
  • Female camels carry their calves upto 14 months before giving birth
  • When a camel find water,they drink 40 gallons in one go

How to make a chameleon

Interesting facts about chameleons
  • Unlike lizards,chameleons cannot regrow their tails.
  • Half of their species live in Madagascar.
  • Their eyes are independent, which means that they can in different directions at the same time.
  • Chameleons aren't deaf,but they don't have ear openings.
  • The size of chameleons can range from 15mm(0.59 in) to 69 cm(27 in).

How to make a pigeon

Interesting facts about pigeons
  • Pigeons are living since 3000 BC
  • Pigeons are always seens in flocks of 20-30 pigeons
  • We never see baby pigeons because after coming out of the eggs,they stay for 2 months in the nest
  • Pigeons are fed by Sikhs in the memory of Guru Gobind Singh,,who loved pigeons.
  • Pigeons can fly upto and beyond 6000 feet and their fastest recorded flying speed is 92.5 mph.

How to make a panda

Interesting facts about Panda

  • Pandas' eyes are different than other bears.
  • Pandas have a great camouflage to their environment.
  • Pandas have an extended wrist bone which is used as a thumb to grip food
  • Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours in eating
  • Pandas need at least 2 bamboos to prevent starvation
